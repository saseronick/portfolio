B2B SaaS Service Desgn

200,000 corporate banking customers were seamlessly transitioned onto a new SaaS communications hub

Opportunity

A large American financial services enterprise intended to merge three similar but distinct business-to-business digital financial products and their associated internal service, support, and delivery models into a single communications-based component of their overall software-as-a-service, offered to corporate finance offices, globally. Historically, communication with customers had not been well-received, if heard, and so a strategic content strategy and communication strategy was required for both the changes and the communication of those changes.

  • Semi-structured user/stakeholder interviews
  • Process Design/Business Strategy
  • Current-state and future-state service blueprints
  • Presentation Design + Achieving Buy-In
  • Discovery phase and Delivery strategy

Front stage:
This change in the service experience for existing enterprise customers would necessarily differ from their established mental models. To ensure user satisfaction, the design of the new service would have to be mindful of the needs and expectations of existing service customers. To ensure the teamâ€™s commitment to no increase in customer service calls and zero service interruptions, a phased, multi-channel communication strategy would need to be effectively deployed.

Back stage:
To increase profitability of the business unit, three teams working on products serving similar communication needs were aligned into one product team, redeploying some resources to other parts of the business. Therefore, three similar but unique online services for enterprise customers were to be combined into a single, newly designed service.

Goals

Information Architecture Design

FDesign the new service, ensuring desired functionality from the existing three services is not lost or duplicated.

Agile Delivery Plan

Create a roadmap for the phased production of design and content strategy deliverables.

Executive Stakeholder MAnagement

Achieve required buy-in from all internal stakeholders, including portfolio and product managers across business units and 30 different portfolios.

Content and Communication Strategy

Roll out new service to customers without service availability interruption or increase in customer calls.
